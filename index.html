About Braiding by Mary NYC

We provide expert African hair braiding services with professional stylists and premium quality.

Our Story

Braiding by Mary NYC was founded with a passion for creating beautiful, authentic African-inspired hairstyles. With years of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for excellence in hair braiding services.

Our salon in Harlem has become a trusted destination for clients seeking high-quality braiding services in a welcoming environment.

We take pride in our work and strive to provide every client with a positive experience and results they'll love.

Our Expertise

At Braiding by Mary NYC, we specialize in a wide range of braiding styles, from traditional box braids and cornrows to more intricate styles like Fulani braids, Senegalese twists, and passion twists.

Our skilled stylists have years of experience and continuously update their skills to stay current with the latest trends and techniques.

We use only premium quality hair products to ensure that your style not only looks beautiful but also promotes healthy hair.

Our Commitment to You

We believe that every client deserves personalized attention and care. When you visit Braiding by Mary NYC, you'll receive a consultation to understand your preferences and hair needs. We're committed to ensuring your satisfaction with our services and creating styles that enhance your natural beauty.
